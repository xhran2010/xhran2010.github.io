I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at [The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/), advised by [Prof. Hui Xiong](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=cVDF1tkAAAAJ) and [Prof. Ying Sun](https://sunyinggilly.github.io/). Before that, I received my MPhil degree in Data Science from the [University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)](https://www.ustc.edu.cn/) in 2023, and my B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology from [Hefei University of Technology (HFUT)](https://www.hfut.edu.cn/) in 2020. My research interests include **Recommender Systems**, **Large Language Models**, and **Data Mining**.
